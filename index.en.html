Welcome to Horia

Horia is a philosophy rooted in a better understanding of the world and the human being, and in faith in the One God, named **Hora**. Hora is the God of truth, light, and justice — the source of all goodness and perfection, who bathes existence in endless light and gives meaning to all things. God is beyond all mental images, beyond time and space, calling us to the path of love and reason.

Horia teaches that the human being is a complex, multi-dimensional creature living through three continuous realms:
  1. The Pre-World: a spiritual and transcendent realm where the soul was created and from which it has come.
  2. The Present World: the physical, material world — the realm of experience, struggle, and growth.
  3. The After-World: life after death, a stage of further evolution and the continuation of real existence.
Horia emphasizes that humans are bearers of collective reason and responsible for the dignity of themselves and all beings. It invites us to self-knowledge, to reconnect with the pre-world, and to strive for goodness in the present world in preparation for the world to come.

Horia is both a faith and a way of life — one that calls us to self-awareness, peaceful coexistence, and the spread of justice and kindness. It is a path lit by Hora, leading toward a more human future.
